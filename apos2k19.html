Alpha Phi Omega Spring 2019 Rush Shirt

Challenge Statement

Create a design for the rush shirt of the Spring 2019 Term of Alpha Phi Omega, Iota Phi Chapter.

Solution Statement

My overall goal in creating this shirt design was to keep it subtle; after two years of being a brother of UC Davis’s Alpha Phi Omega chapter, I had noticed that many active members prefer not to broadcast their letters. Thus, I wanted to create a shirt that represented Alpha Phi Omega’s values and principles while also catering to those that wished for a shirt that they would be willing to casually wear around.

The concept behind my design of the greek letters, “ΑΦΩ,” was geometric shapes; circles and triangles were the templates to forming the letters. As for the design on the back, the “X” shaped lines were used to tie the three symbols—the torch on the left being a symbol of leadership as well as a reference the torch of Alpha Phi Omega, the flowering heart in the center that represents friendship, and the knight’s helmet on the right as a metaphor for service—that reference Alpha Phi Omega’s three cardinal principles (Leadership, Friendship, and Service) and the text spelling the name of the fraternity and the year it was established together.
