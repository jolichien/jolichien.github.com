About Me

San Francisco Bay Area


Hi! My name is Joli and I am a designer and digital illustrator with an affinity for vibrant and eyecatching colors.

I graduated from the University of California, Davis (Class of 2020) with a double major in Design and Cinema & Digital Media. While graphic design was my primary focus in school, I’ve picked up UI/UX design and have spent the last few months completing the Google UX Design Certificate.

I pride myself in my dedication to detail and I enjoy creating works that are meaningful in more ways than one. I find inspiration from the world around me and I am constantly looking for opportunities to learn!
