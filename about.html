About Me

San Francisco Bay Area


Hi! My name is Joli and I am a designer and artist with an affinity for vibrant and eyecatching colors.

I graduated from the University of California, Davis (Class of 2020) with a double major in Design and Cinema & Digital Media. While graphic design was my primary focus in school, I’ve picked up UI/UX design on my own time and have spent the last few months of my life completing the Google UX Design Certificate.

I pride myself in my dedication to detail, I am self driven, and I am constantly looking for opportunities to learn!
