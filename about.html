About Me

San Francisco Bay Area

Hi! My name is Joli and graphic design is my ✨passion✨

Or something like that! I graduated from UC Davis in the Class of 2020 with a double major in Design and Cinema & Digital Media. While graphic design is my primary focus, I’ve dabbled in UI/UX design and am motivated to learn more about said field.

Since graduating, I have also reconnected with my artistic roots as an illustrator and rekindled my love for digital art. While my work tends to be simple in style, I pride myself in my dedication to detail. I believe that a little goes a long way and I enjoy creating works that are meaningful in more ways than one. In my work, I aim to capture the essence of my personality while also providing bold solutions to problems and communicating with clarity through an iterative process.

Hobbies: Crocheting, Music, Dancing (if playing Just Dance counts?...)
