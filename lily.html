Lily's Logo

Challenge Statement

Design a flexible logo that represents and suits the client.

Solution Statement

This logo was designed for a friend whose name is Lily. She uses it on her YouTube channel (although inactive) as a profile and trademark of sorts.

The design of the logo is quite simple and easy. The water lily design with its green leaves forms an “L” shape, representing my friend’s name. Meanwhile, the circular border with a triangular indent emulates the shape of a lily pad, which plays on the water lily in the center. The color palette consists of Lily’s (the client) favorite colors (green and pink) and the pastel tones I chose makes the logo appear more feminine and soft.
