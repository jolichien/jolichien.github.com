Jay's Logo

Challenge Statement

Design a logo that suits and represents the client.

Solution Statement

This logo was created for an individual that has the name Jay, and a last name that begins with R. The overall concept is a play on Jay’s name and the bird species, blue jay, along with the transformation of Jay’s initials, JR, and the fact that Jay’s favorite color is blue. Hence, if looked at carefully, the curve that create the back of the bird in the logo creates the shape of a “J,” and the head and belly (along with the curve created from the “J”) of the bird form the shape of an “R.” Jay also tends to prefer simplicity, thus I felt that a simple color scheme of blues and white was fitting.
