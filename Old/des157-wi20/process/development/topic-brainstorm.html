Final Project Personal Brainstorming

Summary

For this project brainstorming, I created a sort of mind map/web of subjects relating to the prompt that I care about and came to mind. Starting with broader subjects (such as Cyberbullying or Privacy), I branched out with issues I saw with said subjects and then tried to think of ways to create some sort of interactive site that would bring attention to said issues. I think the most convenient thing about the internet is that it can be used to spread information very quickly, so I think an interactive site with an educational purpose would be effective. I am still uncertain about what kind of project I want to create, but I am entertaining the idea of an interactive site that works to spread positivity because the internet can be such a negative place.
