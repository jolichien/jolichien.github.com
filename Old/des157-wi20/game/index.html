The Battle of Dice

There are two players. Role the dice to inflict damage on your opponent! The total of the roll is subtracted from the opponent's HP, unless either die comes up as a “one”. If this happens, the attack misses, and it is the other player’s turn. After each roll, the current player can either roll again, (assuming a “one” was not rolled) or if the current player feels that luck is running thin, they can pass to the other player. The last one standing wins!

If you roll two “ones," your opponent's HP is completely restored, so avoid that if you want to win!
