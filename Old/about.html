about

Hi there! My name is Joli and graphic design is my passion.

…Well, I’m only partially joking. I graduated from UC Davis (Go Ags!) this past June (class of 2020) with a double major in Design and Cinema & Digital Media. While graphic design is my primary focus, I’ve dabbled in UI/UX design and am motivated to learn more about said field.

While my work tends to be simple in style, I pride myself in my dedication to detail. I believe that a little goes a long way and I enjoy creating works that are meaningful in more ways than one. In my work, I aim to capture the essence of my identity while also providing bold solutions to problems and communicating with clarity through an iterative process.
