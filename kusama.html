Yayoi Kusama's Infinity Mirrors

Challenge Statement

Create a poster for an event at a museum, performing arts or other appropriate venue featuring the work of a leader in the arts or sciences. The poster should reflect the legacy of the life’s work of the subject as an event such as a retrospective exhibit in a museum of art, history, or science or some presentation of their work and legacy. It may be a national, international, or smaller exhibition/performance space. Use color in the poster.

Solution Statement

For this project, the event that I researched was the Infinity Mirrors exhibition by Yayoi Kusama that was to be features in The Broad in LA. I have actually personally been to a different museum exhibition of Yayoi Kusama’s works so I have experienced her art first hand. Kusama is know for her fixation on polka dots and many if not most of her works are filled with polka dots.

In my personal experience with Kusama’s exhibitions, there is an interactive portion in which visitors are given a sheet of colorful dot stickers and are invited into a white room filled with white furniture where they can freely place said stickers throughout the room. My poster design for the exhibition in The Broad is inspired by this interactive experience. The colorful dots on my white poster emulate the colorful chaos of dots that I observed in the white room, and the cascading effect of the dots direct the viewer’s eyes towards the title and artist of the exhibition.
