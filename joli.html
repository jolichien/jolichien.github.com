Personal Logo

Challenge Statement

Design a logo that suits my own needs.

Solution Statement

This logo is a play on a pun with my own name; "Joli" sounds similar to "jelly" thus "jolifish" was the basis of this design. The shape of the jellyfish is clear and the tentacles of the jellyfish, if looked at carefully, spells out my name. The logo's color scheme consists of two of my favorite colors, pink and blue. It was originally designed it to identify myself on my music YouTube channel (like the logo design that I created for my friend Lily).
