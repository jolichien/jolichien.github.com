Rainboba

Challenge Statement

Create a sticker design that is appealing to college students.

Solution Statement

The original purpose of this project was to create stickers for my university organization, Alpha Phi Omega, in order to fundraise for our chapter. I chose boba tea as the subject of this design as it is a very trendy and popular drink among college students. The color palettes I chose are pastel and easy on the eyes, each color scheme representing a different flavor of tea drink: the classic milk tea, taro milk tea, strawberry milk tea, thai tea, and matcha milk tea.

Overall, the design is playful and fun, with three boba pearls subtly spelling “UCD,” short for University of California, Davis (my own school).
