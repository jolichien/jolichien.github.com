Harry's Stag

Challenge Statement

To create a simple and unique Harry Potter design that could potentially be printed on t-shirts or other merchandise.

Solution Statement

This was a project that I created on my own free time. As a fan of Harry Potter myself, I wanted to create a cute design that I have not seen yet.

The design features Harry conjuring his patronus which takes the form of a stag. I used muted blues and purples along with cool gray tones to design the stag along with a white glow which gave it the mystical aesthetic that patronuses have. I decided to place Harry on the stagâ€™s back to create a unique visual interaction that is both cute and creative. The lack of outlines was also a decision I made to create a softer look to the overall design.
