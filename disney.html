Disney Emojis

Challenge Statement

Create an emoji based on the face of a character from any Disney or Pixar feature film.

Solution Statement

These four designs were originally designed for the Disney Emoji Contest. To explore my capabilities, I created four emojis based on four different Disney characters. I mainly based the style of the designs on emojis that Disney already created. And so, each emoji was created while keeping the uniqueness of each character in mind and adheres closely to the original designs of each character while converting said designs into emoji form. To do this, I made use of subtle gradients to create that “3-D” effect that emojis often have. Extra accessories such as Ursula’s shell necklace and Ray’s wings were also added as a nod towards the original character designs.
