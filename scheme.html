Project: Color

Challenge Statement

Illustrate an original costume design on a proportional figure with four different color variations based on color schemes.

Solution Statement

While fashion design has never been my main focus in the field of design, I still really enjoy drawing people and clothes; so for this assignment, I decided to design my figure with a fun party theme in mind, hence flouncy cocktail dress, flowing hair, and balloons in the background.

The four color schemes I chose were complementary, triadic, analogous, and compound, each color scheme aiming to bring out a different aspect of the design. Given the flat looking nature of digital design, I created the effect of shading by inserting darker or lighter shades of specific colors to give the design more depth. Additionally, the use of a gradient and some circular shadows on the floor creates a spotlight that grounds the figure.
